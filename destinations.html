Rann of Kutch

Great Rann of Kutch Full Day Tour From ₹2,500

The Great Rann of Kutch is a seasonal salt marsh located in the Thar Desert in the Kutch District of Gujarat, India. It is reputed to be one of the largest salt deserts in the world. This unique landscape transforms with the seasons - a white salt desert during the dry season and a shallow wetland during the monsoon.

Best Time to Visit: October to March, especially during the full moon nights when the white desert glows under moonlight.

Highlights:

  • White salt desert stretching to the horizon
  • Rann Utsav - the cultural festival (Nov-Feb)
  • Full moon night experiences
  • Traditional Kutchi culture and handicrafts
  • Sunset and sunrise views

Dholavira

Dholavira, Kutch Full Day Tour From ₹3,000

Dholavira is one of the five largest Harappan sites and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This ancient city showcases the sophisticated urban planning and water management systems of the Indus Valley Civilization, dating back over 4500 years.

Best Time to Visit: October to March (avoid summer months)

Highlights:

  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Ancient Harappan civilization ruins
  • Sophisticated water management systems
  • Archaeological museum
  • Fossil Park nearby
  • Unique stone architecture
